How It Works

Four simple steps from consultation to results. The entire process is virtual — no office visits, no waiting rooms.

Not sure which program is right? Book a Discovery Consult for $49. Applied toward enrollment if you start a program.

1

Complete Your Virtual Consultation

Click "Start My Program" below to begin your virtual physician consultation through our secure Qualiphy platform. A licensed physician reviews your health history, current medications, and goals — all from your phone or computer.

The consultation is available 7 days a week. No appointment scheduling needed — start when you're ready. Your physician will determine the right program and dosing protocol for your specific needs.

2

Your Physician Prescribes

After reviewing your evaluation, your physician creates a personalized care plan. If appropriate, your prescription is sent directly to our licensed compounding pharmacy — PATH Peptides for peptide programs, or OptioRx for GLP-1 medications.

Compounded medications are not FDA-approved. Prepared by licensed 503A/503B pharmacies.

All prescriptions are patient-specific and overseen by Dr. Joshua Yang, MD, our Medical Director. If labwork is needed, your physician will order it during the consultation and guide you through next steps.

3

Ships Directly to Your Door

Your pharmaceutical-grade medication ships directly from our licensed pharmacy to your home within 3 business days. Everything you need is included — medication, reconstitution supplies (for peptides), syringes, alcohol swabs, and detailed instructions.

Compounded medications are not FDA-approved. Prepared by licensed 503A/503B pharmacies.

Peptides are lyophilized for stability and supported by third-party testing; Certificates of Analysis (COAs) are available on request.

4

Results — With Ongoing Support

Follow your personalized protocol and track how you feel over time. Many patients notice changes within 2–4 weeks — better sleep, improved energy, faster recovery, or weight change depending on your program.

Results may vary. Individual outcomes depend on health history, adherence, and physician recommendations.

You'll receive check-ins at days 3, 7, 14, and 30. Our care team is available throughout your program for logistics and coordination. At day 80, you'll receive a renewal prompt to continue your progress seamlessly.
