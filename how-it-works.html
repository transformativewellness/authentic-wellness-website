What to Expect

Before your consultation

Have a list of your current medications, any relevant medical conditions, and your health goals ready. The more your physician knows, the better your care plan will be.

During your consultation

Your physician reviews your health history, discusses your goals, and recommends the best program. The visit happens entirely on your phone or computer through the secure Qualiphy platform.

After your consultation

If approved, your prescription goes directly to the pharmacy. You'll receive a confirmation with tracking information. Your medication typically arrives within 3 business days, along with all supplies and instructions.

Ongoing care

We check in at days 3, 7, and 30. You can reach our care team anytime. At month 3, your renewal consultation ensures continued progress with adjusted dosing if needed.
