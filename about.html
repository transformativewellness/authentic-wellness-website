About Authentic Wellness

Physician-prescribed performance medicine — backed by science, built for people who take their health seriously.

Our Mission

Authentic Wellness exists because we believe pharmaceutical-grade medicine shouldn't require a waiting room. We are a physician-prescribed telehealth brand offering performance peptide programs and GLP-1 support — fully virtual, shipped directly to your door, with independent licensed physician evaluations through Qualiphy.

Authentic Wellness is not a medical practice. We facilitate access to independent licensed physicians via Qualiphy.

We built AW for the people who read the research. Who ask "why" before taking anything. Who want credible medical oversight without the clinic overhead. Every program is physician-prescribed, medications are prepared by licensed 503A/503B pharmacies, and every patient has direct access to our care team for coordination and support.

Optimized from the inside out.

Our Story

Authentic Wellness was founded by Mckenzie Chavez and her mother — a mother-daughter team who wanted to bring physician-prescribed performance programs to real people, without the clinic markup or the gatekeeping. Every program is overseen by Dr. Joshua Yang, MD, who reviews each patient individually.

Authentic Wellness grew out of Transformative Wellness — a physician-directed med spa in Vista, California, founded by Mckenzie and her mother. As a mother-daughter team, they built TW on a simple idea: clinical excellence should feel personal, not transactional.

Patients kept asking for the same thing — a way to continue their results at home. Peptide therapy, GLP-1 programs, longevity protocols — all without the clinic visit. So we built Authentic Wellness to deliver exactly that: the same physician oversight, the same pharmaceutical-grade compounds, the same clinical standards — fully virtual, shipped nationwide.

AW is a different brand with the same DNA. Same medical director. Same commitment to doing things the right way. Just optimized for the people who want performance medicine on their terms.
