About the Compounds

What you're taking and how it works.

CJC-1295 and Ipamorelin are growth hormone-releasing peptides that work synergistically to stimulate your body's natural growth hormone production. CJC-1295 extends the duration of growth hormone release, while Ipamorelin triggers the pituitary gland to release it. Together, they support lean muscle development, faster recovery, improved body composition, and better sleep — without the risks associated with synthetic HGH.
Compounded medications are not FDA-approved. Prepared by a licensed U.S. pharmacy.
Sermorelin is a growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) analog. It stimulates your pituitary gland to naturally produce and release growth hormone. It's often considered a gentler entry point to peptide therapy, supporting deeper sleep, improved recovery, enhanced skin quality, and overall vitality. Most patients notice sleep improvements within the first 1-2 weeks.
Compounded medications are not FDA-approved. Prepared by a licensed U.S. pharmacy.
NAD+ (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in every cell of your body. It's essential for mitochondrial energy production, DNA repair, and cellular signaling. NAD+ levels decline significantly with age — by your 40s, levels can be roughly half of what they were in your 20s. Supplementing NAD+ supports sustained energy, mental clarity, cellular repair, and healthy aging at the molecular level.
Compounded medications are not FDA-approved. Prepared by a licensed U.S. pharmacy.
GLP-1 receptor agonists (semaglutide and tirzepatide) mimic the incretin hormone GLP-1 in your body. They help regulate appetite by signaling fullness to your brain, slow gastric emptying so you feel satisfied longer, and improve insulin sensitivity. When combined with lifestyle modifications, many people see meaningful weight change. Your physician determines the appropriate compound and dosing based on your individual evaluation.
Compounded medications are not FDA-approved. Prepared by a licensed U.S. pharmacy.

Shipping & Logistics

How your medication gets from pharmacy to your door.

After your physician prescribes your program, your medication ships directly to your door. Most orders ship within 1–2 business days of physician approval and arrive within a few days. Cold-chain shipping is used when required to maintain compound stability.
We ship to patients across the United States. Qualiphy verifies state availability to support telehealth and prescribing compliance. Broader eligibility also depends on your specific medication and state rules — your physician and pharmacy confirm.
Storage instructions vary by compound. Generally, unreconstituted (unmixed) peptide vials can be stored at room temperature or refrigerated. Once reconstituted, peptides should be refrigerated and used within the timeframe specified in your instructions — typically 4-6 weeks. GLP-1 medications are often refrigerated; follow the instructions included with your shipment. Compounded medications are not FDA-approved. Prepared by a licensed U.S. pharmacy.
