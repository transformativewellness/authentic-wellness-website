About the Compounds

What you're taking and how it works.

CJC-1295 and Ipamorelin are growth hormone-releasing peptides that work synergistically to stimulate your body's natural growth hormone production. CJC-1295 extends the duration of growth hormone release, while Ipamorelin triggers the pituitary gland to release it. Together, they support lean muscle development, faster recovery, improved body composition, and better sleep — without the risks associated with synthetic HGH.

Sermorelin is a growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) analog. It stimulates your pituitary gland to naturally produce and release growth hormone. It's often considered a gentler entry point to peptide therapy, supporting deeper sleep, improved recovery, enhanced skin quality, and overall vitality. Most patients notice sleep improvements within the first 1-2 weeks.

NAD+ (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in every cell of your body. It's essential for mitochondrial energy production, DNA repair, and cellular signaling. NAD+ levels decline significantly with age — by your 40s, levels can be roughly half of what they were in your 20s. Supplementing NAD+ supports sustained energy, mental clarity, cellular repair, and healthy aging at the molecular level.

GLP-1 receptor agonists (semaglutide and tirzepatide) mimic the incretin hormone GLP-1 in your body. They help regulate appetite by signaling fullness to your brain, slow gastric emptying so you feel satisfied longer, and improve insulin sensitivity. When combined with lifestyle modifications, many people see meaningful weight change. Your physician determines the appropriate compound and dosing based on your individual evaluation.

Shipping & Logistics

How your medication gets from pharmacy to your door.

After your physician prescribes your program, your medication ships directly from our licensed compounding pharmacy to your home. Peptide programs ship from PATH Peptides and GLP-1 medications ship from OptioRx. Standard delivery is within 3 business days. Cold-chain shipping is used when required to maintain compound stability.
We ship to patients across the United States. Your Qualiphy telehealth consultation includes state verification to ensure compliance with your state's telehealth and prescribing regulations. The consultation platform will confirm availability in your state before you begin.
Storage instructions vary by compound. Generally, unreconstituted (unmixed) peptide vials can be stored at room temperature or refrigerated. Once reconstituted, peptides should be refrigerated and used within the timeframe specified in your instructions — typically 4-6 weeks. GLP-1 medications are often refrigerated; follow the instructions included with your shipment. Compounded medications are not FDA-approved. Prepared by licensed 503A/503B pharmacies.

California Compliance

How we stay compliant with state and federal law.

Yes. California law permits physicians to prescribe medications via telehealth after conducting an appropriate evaluation. Our Qualiphy platform facilitates a synchronous (real-time) video or audio consultation where a licensed physician reviews your health history, conducts the evaluation, and determines whether a prescription is appropriate. This meets the standard for establishing a physician-patient relationship under California telehealth law.
Yes. Compounded medications are legal under Section 503A and 503B of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act. Section 503A pharmacies prepare patient-specific prescriptions based on a physician's order. Our pharmacy partners operate under full state and federal licensing. Every prescription issued through Authentic Wellness is patient-specific — we do not use pre-made or office-stock medications.
A Good Faith Exam is the physician evaluation required before prescribing any medication. During your virtual consultation, the physician reviews your health history, current medications, contraindications, and health goals. This helps the physician determine whether a medication is appropriate for you specifically. It's the same standard of care you'd receive in an in-person visit, delivered via telehealth.
