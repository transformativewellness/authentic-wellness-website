Who we are

Authentic Wellness is operated by Evolving Wellness LLC (“Authentic Wellness,” “we,” “us”). Authentic Wellness is not a medical practice. We facilitate access to independent licensed physicians via Qualiphy.

Contact: info@authenticwellness.com

What information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as name, contact information, date of birth, state of residence, payment details (processed by our payment processor), and information you submit through intake forms.

We automatically collect limited technical data when you use our website, such as device/browser type, IP address, pages viewed, and referral URLs, through cookies and similar technologies.

How we use information

  • To operate the website and respond to inquiries
  • To facilitate telehealth scheduling and physician review through Qualiphy
  • To coordinate pharmacy fulfillment and shipment logistics
  • To process payments and prevent fraud
  • To send service-related messages (account, scheduling, shipping)
  • To send marketing communications when permitted (you can opt out)
  • To improve our website and measure performance

HIPAA-related notice

Certain information you provide in connection with telehealth services may be protected health information (“PHI”) under HIPAA when created or received by a HIPAA-covered entity or business associate.

Authentic Wellness may receive or handle PHI only as permitted by law and applicable agreements. Your telehealth evaluation is conducted through Qualiphy, and your treating clinician and their organization are responsible for their own HIPAA compliance obligations.

If you have questions about how your clinician uses or shares PHI, request their Notice of Privacy Practices.

Service providers (disclosures)

We use service providers to run our operations. Depending on your interactions, this may include:

  • Qualiphy for telehealth scheduling, disclosures, and physician evaluation workflows
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • GoHighLevel (GHL) for CRM, SMS/email automation, and marketing workflows
  • Pharmacy partners (for example PATH Peptides and OptioRx) for dispensing and shipping
  • Hosting/analytics providers that help us operate and measure the website

We share information with service providers only as needed for them to perform services on our behalf, subject to appropriate safeguards and contracts where required.

Sale of personal information

We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies

We use essential cookies required for site functionality. We may use optional analytics or marketing cookies depending on your choices and applicable law.
